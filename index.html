About Me

Muralikrishna SN , holds Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) formerly known as Manipal University. He was conferred the doctoral degree for the thesis titled ‘Development of Novel Algorithms for Human Action and Activity Recognition in Video Surveillance Systems’ under the guidance of Dr. Balachandra and Dr. U Dinesh Acharya, Manipal Insitute of Technology, MAHE, Manipal, India. In the past, he has worked on several relevant projects in the field of Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing (NLP) in various capacities as principal investigator (PI), Co-investigator (Co-PI), and as team member. Some of my recent works include Human Activity Recognition, Video Background Subtraction, and Natural Language Processing in the healthcare domain.
